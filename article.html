Juna Affiliate program works as similar to Adsterra Affiliate. You get your Store ID then start your affiliate program.

To start your affiliate program follow these steps. Get a Store ID. You can a multiple ID but not with the same Email account.

Click on the Affiliate icon to select among the product you would like advertise for Juna Store. Check out for the high commission you want. Click and submit to Juna Store Affiliate for your product identification.
