Juna Affiliate program works as similar to Adsterra Affiliate. You get your Store ID then start your affiliate program. To start your affiliate program follow these steps. Get a Store ID. You can a multiple ID but not with the same Email account. Click on the Affiliate icon to select among the product you would like advertise for Juna Store. Check out for the high commission you want. Click and submit to Juna Store Affiliate for your product identification. If no product is found. Sound to Juna Store to request for the list of Affiliate they are having. You can use the same store ID for selling g and for affiliate purposes. Your commission is recorded in your Selling Portal after your KYC had been verified. Note: You need to verify your KYC to be able to gain access to all platforms. After your KYC had been verified, a platform website will be sent to your email.
